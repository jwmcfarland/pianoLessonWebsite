ABOUT JACOB

Hello! My name is Jacob McFarland, from an early age I've had a passion for music. I was fortunate to have amazing local teachers and started taking piano lessons in kindergarden. With the exellect guidance from my parents and teachers throughout the years I have been able to continue to grow as a musician and a person. I was classically trained and started learning jazz once I joined the Jr. High jazz band. From there I was hooked and continued to pursue jazz piano and was fortunate to be able to study under Dameon Place, Dameon Place Dick Redman, Nick Leo, Susie Miget. I want to help pass on the passion of music and help other achieve their goals of learning an instrument, send me a message below if you want to get started!
